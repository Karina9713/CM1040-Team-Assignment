Park Attractions

Wonder World Amusement Park has a variety of attractions for all ages. There are rides, shows, and exhibits that will entertain and educate you. You can explore the park and discover the many wonders of the world.

Space Station: A futuristic ride that simulates a journey to outer space. You can experience zero gravity, see the stars and planets, and encounter aliens and asteroids.

Time Machine: A historical ride that transports you to different eras and places. You can witness the dinosaurs, the pyramids, the medieval castles, and the modern cities.

Dimension Portal: A fantastical ride that opens a gateway to other realms and dimensions. You can visit the fairyland, the underworld, the dreamworld, and the nightmare world.

Ocean Explorer: A natural ride that dives into the depths of the ocean. You can see the coral reefs, the shipwrecks, the marine life, and the underwater volcanoes.
